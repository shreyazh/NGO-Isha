About ISHA

ISHA - Ichha Sahayta Hito Anukool represents our unwavering commitment to creating positive change in our communities. Founded on the principles of compassion, dignity, and empowerment, we work tirelessly to address the most pressing social challenges of our time.

Our mission is rooted in the belief that every individual deserves access to basic necessities, education, and opportunities for growth. Through our comprehensive programs, we aim to break the cycles of poverty, homelessness, and inequality that affect millions of lives.

Our Programs

Comprehensive initiatives designed to create lasting change in communities across multiple sectors.

🏠

Homeless Rehabilitation

Our homeless support program provides immediate shelter, nutritious meals, medical care, and long-term rehabilitation services. We work with local shelters and create temporary housing solutions while helping individuals develop job skills and secure permanent housing.

👶

Child Welfare & Education

Focused on breaking the cycle of poverty through education, we provide school supplies, uniforms, tutoring services, and after-school programs. Our child welfare initiatives ensure proper nutrition, healthcare, and protection for vulnerable children.

💻

Digital Literacy Initiative

Providing tablets, smartphones, and internet access to underprivileged families. Our program includes digital skills training, online education resources, and tech support to help bridge the digital divide in underserved communities.

🩺

Women's Health & Hygiene

Distribution of menstrual hygiene products, health education workshops, and reproductive health services. We work to eliminate period poverty and ensure women have access to essential health resources and information.

📰

Community Journalism

Supporting local journalists and media initiatives to ensure important community stories are told. We provide training, equipment, and platforms for citizen journalism, promoting transparency and community engagement.

🌱

Sustainable Development

Environmental awareness programs, sustainable livelihood training, and community garden projects. We focus on creating long-term solutions that benefit both people and the planet.

Our Impact

Measuring success through the lives we've touched and the communities we've transformed.

Education Transformation

Over 15,000 children now have access to quality education through our programs. We've established 50 learning centers and provided digital devices to 8,000 students, significantly improving literacy rates in target communities.

Homeless Rehabilitation

Successfully helped 2,500+ individuals transition from homelessness to stable housing. Our job training programs have achieved an 85% employment rate among participants, breaking the cycle of homelessness.

Women's Health Initiative

Distributed over 100,000 menstrual hygiene kits and reached 25,000 women with health education programs. School dropout rates among girls in our target areas decreased by 40%.

Digital Inclusion

Provided 5,000 tablets and trained 12,000 individuals in digital literacy. Our programs have enabled 3,000+ people to access online education and job opportunities, bridging the digital divide.
